How It Works

If you see trash on the ground, pick it up and throw it away! Snap a picture while you are doing it, then upload it to Instagram or Twitter with the hashtag #keepindyclean.

Every picture you post enters you to win a random drawing at the end of each month! Prizes include Apple Watches, Rokus, Fitbits, Beats Headphones, GoPros, Pebbles, Bluetooth Speakers, iPads, and more!

What are you waiting for? Get out there and help us #keepindyclean!
